What Gauged does

Everything your car deserves to have documented.

Built for people who actually care about their cars. Not a search engine. Not a forum. A structured, searchable record of everything that matters.

Parts database

OEM part numbers, supersession chains, aftermarket cross-references, and fitment data for every model. Shops and enthusiasts both.

Vehicle history

Mileage verification, title checks, accident records, service history, and ownership timeline. Know exactly what you're buying.

Market value

Real-time pricing from active listings, auction results, and private sales. Know what your car is worth and when to buy or sell.

Common faults

NHTSA complaints, TSBs, and owner-reported issues ranked by frequency and severity. Know what breaks before it breaks.

Year vs year

Every change between model years, clearly laid out. Added, improved, removed, unchanged. Know if the upgrade is actually worth it.

Service finder

Find shops that specialize in your exact model, verified by owners who've actually been there. Filter by fault expertise and location.

Crash test ratings

NHTSA and IIHS results in plain language, with test videos. Understand what the ratings actually mean for real-world safety.

Aftermarket mods

Most-installed mods ranked by owner adoption, with real dyno numbers and install difficulty ratings from the community.

Owner builds

A living record of every mod, every issue, every mile. Log your build, follow others, and contribute to the community database.
